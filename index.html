Galaxy Chocolate

About Galaxy Chocolate

Galaxy is a brand of milk chocolate made and marketed by Mars, Incorporated. It was first introduced in the United Kingdom in 1960.

Interesting Facts

  • Galaxy chocolate is known for its smooth and creamy texture.
  • The brand is also known as Dove in some countries, including the United States.
  • Galaxy chocolate bars come in various flavors, including milk chocolate, caramel, and hazelnut.

Nutritional Information

One serving of Galaxy chocolate (25g) contains approximately:

  • Calories: 130
  • Total Fat: 7g
  • Saturated Fat: 4.5g
  • Cholesterol: 5mg
  • Sodium: 20mg
  • Total Carbohydrates: 16g
  • Sugars: 16g
  • Protein: 1g
